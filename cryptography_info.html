Elliptic Curve Cryptography

  • Generate a private and public key

  • Type the message you want to encrypt and the public key of the reciever

  • Send the encrypted text to the reciever

  • To decrypt a message sent to you, type your private key and paste the encrypted message

  • Because of Javascript's upper bound to "safe" arithmetic, encryption and decryption are slow (it uses BigInt arithmetic, which is a lot slower).

  • A message of more than two characters will be broken down in smaller messages of two characters each. Because of the above problem, small messages are recommended.

  • The parameters are:

    • prime = 16777259
    • y² = x³ + 3x + 7
    • base point = [2699101: 9795704:1]
