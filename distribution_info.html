Elliptic Curve Distribution

  • Given a prime p, it is known that the possible cardinalities of rational points on an elliptic curve lie in a closed interval.

  • We can then count the number of elliptic curves with a given number of rational points (always over the prime field given by p).

  • The prime p is not tested to be prime (check for a non-prime p and see what happens).
